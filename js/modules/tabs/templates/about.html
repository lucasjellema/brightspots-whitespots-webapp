About the Brightspots & Whitespots Dashboard

Purpose

The Brightspots & Whitespots Dashboard is a tool designed to visualize and analyze survey data regarding perceived upcoming trends, technologies and customer challenges and requests for help. It helps us identify both areas of strength ("brightspots") and opportunities for improvement ("whitespots") in technology adoption, customer challenges, and emerging trends.

Survey Methodology

The underlying survey collects data from various companies and professionals across our ecosystem to understand:

  • Key customer challenges and pain points as observed at customers
  • Emerging technology adoption patterns
  • Technology trends and vendor landscape

What are "Brightspots"?

Brightspots represent areas where our ecosystem of expert companies provides a service portfolio to match the customer demand. These are opportunities where our sales organization can leverage existing propositions and delivery strengths and knowledge.

What are "Whitespots"?

Whitespots are areas of customer demand or emerging opportunities where we currently do not have the existing offerings or even enough clear roadmap and ambition to meet the (upcoming) customer demand. These are areas where organizations might want to focus their innovation efforts or learning initiatives.

How to Use This Dashboard

This dashboard provides multiple perspectives on the survey data:

  • Overview: High-level summary of survey responses and key findings
  • Customer Themes: Analysis of primary customer challenges and concerns
  • Emerging Tech: Exploration of emerging technology concepts and their adoption
  • Technology Trends: Insights into technology products and vendors
  • Companies: Expert Company-specific views and claims & ambitions
  • People: Role-based analysis of customer challenges, emerging technology interests and focus areas

Data Sources

The data displayed in this dashboard is from the initial survey among members of the CAB, CTOs across Europe and Client Directors.
